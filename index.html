Juhi Thakore is a student at the University of Florida.

The future is extremely uncertain for her. She knows she'll be a software engineer, if for no other reason than that's what she's been trained to do, but hopes that she'll one day get the opportunity to explore the amazing world of project management.

Most of her hobbies consist of exercising her creativity in new ways. She has taken up and dropped so many different hobbies that composing a list would be futile.

Some of the hobbies which have remained consistent for many years include reading, doodling, and baking. Some of the less consistent hobbies include photography, cooking, and writing. All photos on this web-page were taken by Juhi and she hopes you like them.
